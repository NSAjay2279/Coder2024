CS50: Introduction to Computer Science

About the Course

CS50 is an introductory course in computer science that teaches the fundamentals of programming and problem-solving. Through a series of interactive lectures, hands-on tutorials, and real-world projects, students will gain a strong foundation in the principles of computer science and the art of programming.

What You'll Learn

  • Problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Software design and architecture
  • Algorithmics and data structures
  • Object-oriented programming
  • Web development

Who Should Take This Course

CS50 is designed for anyone with an interest in learning about computer science. No prior programming experience is required. This course is a great introduction for high school students, college undergraduates, and adults who are looking to learn the basics of computer science.

Prerequisites

No prior programming experience is required. However, a basic understanding of mathematics, such as algebra and geometry, is helpful.
